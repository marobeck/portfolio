# Schedule Companion

## Overview

Worked with a team to design a productivity device that utilizes a dedicated tablet device and a synchronous web application that is designed to assist with organizing a user's activities on the basis of necessity, urgency, and frequency.

![Render](schedule_companion_render.png)

## Motivation

Schedduling applications lack the medium to exercise greater precedence over other application and often lack organization between activities on the basis of necessity, urgency, and frequency.
